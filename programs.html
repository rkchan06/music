Toronto Music Camp close

For more information, click here

Toronto Music Camp (TMC) is a day camp that teaches kids how to work together as musicians while recording and performing the style music they love. It is an escape from the isolation of private music lessons over the school year, where kids come around the world to attend TMC to develop musicianship and have fun. Unlike other March Break and Summer camps in Toronto, their groups are small so all campers receive the individualized attention they deserve while rehearsing with their band or troupe.

The summer camp has an enrollment fee of $499+tax for registration plus an additional discount on each week if two or more sessions are chosen

Cosmo Music Camp is a day camp that has a variety of music camps for students of all ages. Their camps are all led by professional musicians and are held in our fully-equipped music studios and performance halls. This years camp is the instrumental discovery camp which is designed for children to explore various instruments before committing to one. Over the first week, campers get to play many instruments and work with our instructors to choose their favourites for week two. In week two, they sharpen their skills to prepare for their debut performance on stage! This camp includes outdoor time.

The summer camp runs for 2 weeks long and has an enrollment fee of $795+tax. Early drop-off available from 8am to 9am for $60 per session and extended care is available from 4pm to 5:30pm for $120 per session.

TSSP Campsclose

For more information, click here

Toronto School for Strings and Piano camps are day camps for ages 4-12 that emphasize the quality of the learning environment and foster artistic growth among all their students. Instruments taught include violin, viola, cello, bass, piano, guitar, ukulele and electric bass. They encourage their students to take advantage of enriching opportunities such as rehearsing with a professional accompanist or playing with small ensembles or orchestra. TSSP offers a fun and creative approach to theory in private lessons or small groups. They also encourage the implementation of music technology. The core of their instruction is classical by nature, but they also include jazz and improvisation.

Their summer camps are 1 week long, with the option of half day or full day. A full day is $485+tax and a half day is $295+tax

Their fee is inclusive of a half or full day camp program. No food provided. HST extra.

1585 Yonge St, Toronto ON, M4T 1Z9, Canada
