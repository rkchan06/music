With the Ontario Music Grant being cut by more than half, young students lack the musical education they require. Studies have shown that playing a musical instrument makes you smarter as it changes the shape and power of the brain, improving cognitive skills.

Our mission is to provide musical opportunites and support these music programs around the GTA in order to improve the accessibility to music for youth. Your donation allows us to help provide these programs and support local music activities and charities. All donations help and are greatly appreciated.
